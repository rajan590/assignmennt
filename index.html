Description of Avaiable Faculty in Oxford

BCA

A Bachelor in Computer Application (BCA) is a popular course for students interested in the computer field. The course aims to develop students' skills and knowledge in programming and software development. It also provides students with practical and theoretical experience in computer application studies.

BBA

A Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) is a three-year undergraduate program that prepares students for careers in business management and leadership. BBA programs cover a wide range of topics, including: marketing, finance, human resources, operations management, entrepreneurship, and information technology

BHM

A Bachelor of Hotel Management (BHM) course in Nepal teaches students how to manage hotels, restaurants, resorts, and other hospitality businesses: Curriculum BHM courses cover a variety of topics, including food production, front office operations, housekeeping, and hotel engineering. Students also take compulsory courses in English, economics, statistics, and more.
